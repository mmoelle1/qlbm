qlbm.tools.utils.qiskit_circuit_to_qulacs(circuit)[source]#

Converts a Qiskit QuantumCircuit to a Qulacs QuantumCircuit. Conversion takes place by first converting the Qiskit circuit into its OpenQASM 2 representation. This representation is then parsed into a Qulacs circuit.

Parameters:

circuit (QiskitQC) – The Qiskit QuantumCircuit to convert.

Returns:

The Qulacs counterpart to the QuantumCircuit.
